Eight Sustainable Living

Conceptual design of a new sustainable living system to reduce packaging waste, specifically in the food produce industry. Collaboration with Alberto Montemiglio and Ashley Xu. Winner of Schneider Go Green Building Award UK.

brief

Our task was to analyse the existing state of packaging within the food produce industry and identify key sustainability issues. From this, we would design and propose a future solution to improve sustainability and promote a more circular system.

outcomes

Eight buildings is a circular economy, self-sufficient living solution projected for 2040 validated by foresight research and horizons scoping. The final concept consists of food grown via aeroponic technology, meat cultured on-site and packaged with bioplastic film as well as an underground biofuel plant for waste treatment.

Date: 2019
Main: foresight research, system design, graphic presentation/visualisation, meat culture & aeroponic research
