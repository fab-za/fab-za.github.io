Sewing & Crafts Table

A table designed for crafts and sewing enthusiasts. Yes, that's one of my beloved cats occupying the shelf on the day it was set up.

Date: 2018
Individual Project

brief

To design a solution for client based on interview conducted below:
"Currently, our sewing equipment is stored in separate areas of the house, both upstairs and downstairs and most of it is not located near where the sewing machine is. Hence, I need a storage space where I can keep items such as fabrics and thread spools all in one place. Also, there is a need for a table with a large surface for sewing."

outcomes

An 800x1100x820mm table consisting of drawers with built-in dividers, a specialised hanging storage for thread spools, three spacious shelves for supplies and an underlying geometric design theme present in the unique metal leg frame and spool storage. The project was conducted individually from initial project scoping and formulation of specification to CAD designs to manufacturing of physical final product.
